About Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India in the 17th century. He was known for his progressive administration, military strategies, and vision of a united India. His contributions to Indian society and his valiant efforts against the Mughal Empire are celebrated to this day.
